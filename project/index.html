simple omelette recipe

an easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal this classic omelette combines beaten eggs coocked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheesse, vegatables, or meats.

preporation time

  • total:approximately 1o minutes
  • preporation:5 minutes
  • cooking:5 minutes

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or all
  • optional fillings cheese,diced vegatables,coocked meats,herbs

instructions

  1. beat the eggs: in a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed,you can add a tablespoon of water or milk to make a fluffier texture.
  2. heat the pan: place a non-stick frying pan over heat and add butter or oil.
  3. cook the omelette:once the butter is melted and bu,bbling pour in the eggs,and tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. add fillings(optional):when the eggs begin to set adt the edges but are stil slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. hold and serve: as the omelette continues to cook the carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings,let it cook for another minute,then slide it onto the plate.
  6. enjoy:serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed
